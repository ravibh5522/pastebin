Frequently Asked Questions

Is Paste Shaver completely free?

Yes — Paste Shaver is 100% free forever. No credit card, no hidden fees, no ads. Create unlimited pastes, upload files, and use group chat at no cost.

Does Paste Shaver require registration or sign-up?

No registration is required to create or view pastes. Just paste your content and hit Create. Optionally create a free account to save & manage pastes from a dashboard.

How secure is the encryption?

Private pastes use AES-256-GCM encryption — the same standard used by banks and governments. Your PIN is the encryption key and is never stored on our servers. Only someone with your PIN can decrypt the paste.

Can I upload files with my paste?

Yes! You can attach multiple files per paste — images, code files, documents, and more. Files attached to private pastes are also AES-256-GCM encrypted.

Can pastes automatically delete themselves?

Yes. Choose from: 15 minutes, 1 hour, 1 day, 2 days (default), 1 week, 1 month, or never. Expired pastes are permanently deleted automatically.

What programming languages are supported for syntax highlighting?

Paste Shaver auto-detects 100+ languages including Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Go, Rust, Java, C/C++, C#, PHP, Ruby, Swift, Kotlin, SQL, JSON, YAML, Bash, HTML, CSS, and many more.

Is Paste Shaver a good pastebin.com alternative?

Paste Shaver offers several features pastebin.com charges for (encryption, password protection, self-destruct) at zero cost, plus exclusive features like file uploads and real-time group chat — all without ads.

How do I share a paste without needing to code?

No coding needed! Type or paste any text into the box, click Create Paste, then share the link. One click — and your recipient just visits the URL to read it. Use the Share buttons to send to WhatsApp, Telegram, Twitter, or copy to clipboard instantly.
